About Me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Western University, advised by Prof. Charles Ling (Fellow, Canadian Academy of Engineering) and Prof. Boyu Wang.

Prior to this, I received my Master's degree and Bachelor's degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), where I was fortunate to work with Prof. Junqing Yu and Prof. Wei Yang.

Research Interests: My research focuses on adversarial machine learning, including unlearnable examples, adversarial perturbations, and their applications in deep learning security. I am also interested in video understanding, anomaly detection, and generative models.
